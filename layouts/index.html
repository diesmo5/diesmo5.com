Meet

DIESMO5

DIESMO5 is a parent company to multiple companies in specializing in software development, app development, web design, online education, esports, and philanthropy.
Our core principles stem from our fundamental beliefs in education and software. As a two-fold manifesto we believe that education is the key towards progress. Because of this we believe in creating a culture where we are continuously learning as well as educating our clients, customers, and family.
Also, we believe that the world ahead of us will surely revolve around software and hardware connected and integrated into every aspect of our lives. This belief has inspired us to make steps towards educating youth around the globe in the field of computer science and computer engineering. Our products and services also reflect these beliefs, because we focus on empowering others to build their businesses, create apps, websites, games, and help them follow their passion.
