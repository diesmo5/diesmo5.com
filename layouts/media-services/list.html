FAQ

Which platform will you use for my website?
diesmo5 media uses Squarespace because it is flexible, easy to use, and affordable. However, we're also open to using any platform you are more comfortable with.
What costs should I expect other than website design services?
Squarespace: Sign up for the Business plan from Squarespace. This will be an ongoing expense for as long as the website is live. You get a 20% discount on your first year if you work with diesmo5 media.
Domain: If you purchase your domain from Squarespace, it’s free for your first year. I get my domains from Amazon Web Services because they’re so easy to work with and protect my privacy for free.
Privacy Policy: : If you plan on getting people to sign up for freebies by providing their names and email addresses, this is a legal requirement. Unless you already have one, diesmo5 media is open to providing a privacy policy for your website.
